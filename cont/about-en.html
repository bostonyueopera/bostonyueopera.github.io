Yue Opera, also called ShaoXing Opera, is the second most popular opera form out of over 360 opera genres in China. It is highly popular in the lower reaches of the Yangtze River region, while its audiences are all over China. It is also a genre dominated by all-female casting.

The recently formed Boston Yue Opera House (BYOH) is a volunteer based group, serving the communities in the Greater Boston Area. We are dedicated to promote Yue (Shaoxing) Opera, serve communities, enable cultural diversity and grow our fan base in the Northeast region and beyond.

Boston Yue Opera House is a registered 501(c)(3) non-profit organization.
