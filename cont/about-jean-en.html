A Beijing'er, Jean grew up far away from where Yue Opera was originated. She immediately became attached to it after watching the classic movie "Dream of Red Chamber". Followed by "Chasing Mermaid", "Jasper" and many originals, she became more and more fascinating about it, gradually developing a love of Chinese opera arts. Regardless of the genre and style, she possesses a variety of interest in many favorite vocals. She has participated in the Yue Opera performance in Beijing, later became a faculty member at National Academy of Chinese Theatre Arts before she moved to US. Many years have passed by, her love for Yue Opera has not changed a bit.
